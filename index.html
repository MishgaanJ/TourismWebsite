GILGIT

Gilgit is a city located in the Gilgit-Baltistan region of northern Pakistan. Nestled in the Karakoram mountain range, it serves as a gateway to some of the world's highest peaks, including K2. Known for its breathtaking landscapes, Gilgit is a popular destination for trekking, mountaineering, and adventure tourism. The region is also rich in cultural heritage, featuring diverse ethnic groups and historical sites.

MURREE

Murree is a popular hill station located in the Punjab province of Pakistan, near the capital city, Islamabad. Situated in the foothills of the Himalayas, it offers a cool climate and scenic beauty, making it a favored retreat from the summer heat. Known for its lush green hills, pine forests, and picturesque views, Murree attracts tourists year-round. The town features colonial-era architecture, bustling markets, and a variety of recreational activities, including hiking and chairlift rides.
